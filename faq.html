Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I report a lost item?

To report a lost item, simply visit the "Lost" section of our website and fill out the form with a detailed description of your lost item.

2. How do I claim a found item?

To claim a found item, head to the "Found" section and provide the necessary details. If the item is yours, you will be able to schedule a pickup or delivery.

3. What should I do if I found someone’s item?

Please report it under the "Report Found" section. Provide a detailed description and, if possible, upload an image to help the owner identify it.

4. Is there a fee for using this service?

No, our platform is free for users to report lost and found items. However, some delivery services may charge fees for item returns.

5. How long are lost items listed on the website?

Lost items remain listed for 90 days. After that, they are archived but can still be searched through the archives for reference.

6. Can I upload photos when reporting a lost item?

Yes, you can upload images when reporting lost items to help others recognize them more easily.

7. What happens if I report a lost item but later find it?

You can update the status of your report to "Found" by logging into your account and accessing your reports.

8. How do I contact someone who found my item?

Use the contact form available on the item’s page. For privacy, we handle the initial communication to verify both parties.

9. What if I don’t see my lost item listed?

Items are added frequently, so check back regularly. You can also set up alerts to notify you when items matching your description are posted.

10. Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. Personal information is only shared with the owner or finder after verification.
